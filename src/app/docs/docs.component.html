About IJAH Analytics

Ijah analytics is a website that provides predictions of a herbal formula for a disease by connecting plants to compounds, compounds to proteins, and proteins to diseases.

Ijah analytics is inspired by the habits of Indonesian people who use herbal medicine for generations as an alternative treatment that is natural and also inexpensive.

Ijah Analytics makes it easy for herbal medicine researchers to find out the content of active compounds present in herbal plants so as to shorten the process of formulation of herbal products, obtained from the analysis of multipartite graph of plants-protein-disease compounds.

Network Pharmacology can cut the screening step of selecting herbal compounds to be shorter. Computational involvement in the design of herbal medicine becomes a factor that is considered very important. IJAH Analytics provides convenience. Machine learning is the basis of the application development method.

Some features of ijah analytics include drug-target connectivity, cluster connectivity, and compound synergi
