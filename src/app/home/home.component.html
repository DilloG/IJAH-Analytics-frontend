What is IJAH Analytics?

Indonesian Herbal Herbs Analytics or IJAH Analytics is a website that provides information on the connectivity of compounds to proteins in a disease so that they can provide predictions about herbal formulas that are suitable for a disease.

IJAH Analytics is inspired by the habits of Indonesian people who use medicinal plants as a treatment for maintaining health, whose properties are known for generations.
